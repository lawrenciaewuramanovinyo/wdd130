Ghana

Ghana, located in West Africa, is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning landscapes. From the historic castles of Cape Coast to the bustling markets of Accra, Ghana offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity. The country is famous for its warm hospitality, colorful festivals, and delicious cuisine, such as jollof rice and fufu. Ghana is also home to beautiful beaches, lush rainforests, and wildlife reserves, making it a top destination for travelers seeking adventure and cultural experiences.
